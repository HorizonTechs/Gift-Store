Surprise is the greatest gift which life can grant us

Despite the cheer that comes from receiving a gift, there’s way more satisfaction from being the person giving the gift; a feeling which can’t be measured in monetary terms. The joy you get from opening a gift is but instantaneous, however giving provides a more self-satisfying experience that is sustainable.
We learn the importance of giving gifts when we are yet kids when we strive to get good grades to put a smile on the faces of our parents. A simple act but one which provides a tremendous response. It has been shown that giving makes us more joyful than receiving. It doesn’t make a difference if the gift is valuable or not.
